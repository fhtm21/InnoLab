# INNOGEN Lab — Official Information

## What is the INNOGEN Lab?
**INNOGEN Lab** (Innovation Intelligent Knowledge Generation) is a new academic and research facility at **BINUS @Bekasi**, supported by the **School of Information Systems**. It is developed to support innovation related to **AI**, **data science**, **smart systems**, and **Industry 4.0**.

The lab is designed as a multi-purpose space where **students**, **lecturers**, and **research partners** can collaborate, experiment, and build real-world prototypes.

---

## Core purpose
INNOGEN Lab was created to become:

### 1) Teaching & learning
INNOGEN Lab serves as a hands-on learning environment that enhances academic courses through:
- Practical lab sessions for **Machine Learning**, **Deep Learning**, **Modern Analytics**, and related AI subjects
- Hands-on student projects using AI tools and real-world datasets
- Support for innovative coursework across the School of Information Systems

This makes the lab a central place for students to practice, experiment, and master industry-relevant technologies.

### 2) Research & development
The lab supports AI-driven research initiatives by:
- Facilitating research in **data analytics**, **automation**, and **intelligent systems**
- Helping faculty and students develop **publications** and **AI prototypes**
- Enabling integration with AI initiatives and cross-campus collaboration

It serves as a research accelerator, enabling ideas to be tested, validated, and translated into academic outputs or working prototypes.

### 3) Collaboration & impact
INNOGEN Lab is also a collaboration platform that connects BINUS with the broader ecosystem:
- Joint projects with industry partners and international institutions
- Cross-program collaborations to build real-world AI solutions
- Supporting BINUS in building a campus-wide AI-driven learning ecosystem

These collaborations ensure that students and faculty are exposed to current industry needs, global standards, and multidisciplinary teamwork.

---

## Technical capabilities
The lab provides:
- High-performance PCs for **GPU-based deep learning**
- Development environments for **Python**, **TensorFlow**, **PyTorch**, and **Keras**
- Tools for **data engineering**, **IoT simulation**, and **cloud computing**
- Space for collaborative sessions, mentoring, and student supervision

---

## Who is it for?
- Undergraduate and graduate students (Information Systems, Business Analytics, Industrial Engineering, Computer Science/AI)
- Lecturers and researchers across BINUS
- Industry partners seeking applied analytics/AI projects

---

## Why it matters
INNOGEN Lab strengthens BINUS’s mission to become an AI-driven campus, giving students and lecturers a dedicated environment to:
- Build prototypes
- Run research experiments
- Publish scientific papers
- Develop industry-ready skills
- Launch innovation projects

### UN SDG alignment
The INNOGEN Lab project aligns with the following United Nations Sustainable Development Goals (UN SDGs):
- SDG 4 – Quality Education: By providing a dedicated space for hands-on learning and research in AI and data science, the INNOGEN Lab enhances the quality of education at BINUS @Bekasi.
- SDG 8 – Decent Work and Economic Growth: The lab equips students with industry-relevant skills in AI and data science, preparing them for the workforce and contributing to economic growth.
- SDG 9 – Industry, Innovation, and Infrastructure: The lab fosters innovation and supports the development of intelligent systems, contributing to advancements in industry and infrastructure.
- SDG 17 – Partnerships for the Goals: The lab encourages collaboration between BINUS, industry partners, and international institutions, promoting partnerships that drive progress towards sustainable development goals.
